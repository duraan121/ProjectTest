About Us

Our story and philosophy

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Delicious Bistro began as a small family-owned restaurant with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. Our founder, Chef Marco, brought his expertise from Michelin-starred restaurants in Europe to create a menu that blends traditional techniques with local ingredients.

Today, we continue this tradition of excellence, sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farmers and producers. Our team of talented chefs works tirelessly to create dishes that delight both the eyes and the palate.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we've built relationships with trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every dish tells a story, and we're honored to share ours with you.
